Wizarding World

Are you brave and couraged like a Gryffindor, wise and creative like a Ravenclaw, loyal and fair like a Hufflepuff or determined and ambitious like a Slytherin?

When I was 11 I got to read my first Harry Potter book and since then I was a big fan. Growing up with the world of Harry Potter - the Wizarding World - was wonderful and shaped me in a lot of ways. Even though I read a lot as a kid the Harry Potter books brought so much more than just the fun of reading into ones life. There are so many characters, spells, magical creatures and more to learn. Waiting for the next book, the next movie or the next video game in joyful anticipation was common for every fan.

And even today - as the Harry Potter books and movies got to an end - the Wizarding World still continues with many things like the 'Fantastic Beasts' movies, the 'Cursed Child' theater plays or the video game 'Hogwarts Legacy' coming next winter. If you tend to sort everyone you know in one of the Hogwarts houses you know you're pretty deep into it...
